Andy’s First Dictionary， Uva 10815

Problem Description：

输入一个文本,找出所有不同的单词(连续的字母序列),按字典序从小到大输出。单
词不区分大小写。

Sample Input：

Adventures in Disneyland
Two blondes were going to Disneyland when they came to a fork in the
road. The sign read: “Disneyland Left.”
So they went home.

Sample Output ：

（ 为了节约篇幅只保留前5行）

a
adventures
blondes
came
disneyland
